About

Hello there I’m Jameson, a project manager from Medina, Texas. I’m currently looking for project and/or product management opportunities in technology. I graduated with a BA in Economics from The University of Texas at Austin with a minor in Business Foundations from McCombs School of Business. View résumé.,

Starting in the summer of 2012, I worked as a freight broker for a 3rd party logistics provider called FreightPros. Our team was in charge of their entire full truckload operations across United States. We grew our division’s growth and development to a 700% increase in net profits, before I left to start a tech company called Flexter Inc.

Flexter Inc. is a remote development studio focused on making apps for social media influencers. We were selected into Capital Factory's Accelerator Program for pre-seed stage startups where we were exposed to potential angel investors and venture capitalist, office hours with Austin’s most successful entrepreneurs, and much more. My app portfolio has since generated over 200k+ downloads and $200k+ in sales since spring of 2015. I’ve learned a lot from making apps and still run the company in the evenings and weekends.

Most recently, I’ve been working with an IoT start-up in oil & gas. We make small wireless sensors that monitor your oilfield equipment. These sensors range from tanks levels, pressure gauges, beam pumps, and activity monitors. All of which can be self-installed by any gauger in less than 10 minutes. Our disruptive technology gives the low to mid level operators the same capabilities that the big players have for a fraction of the cost.

My goals for 2017 are to become a better writer, find an opportunity to join a design team, and dive into JavaScript web development.
