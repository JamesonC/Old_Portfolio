My Kolbe A Index

Measuring my instinctive method of operation and identifying the way I'm most productive

Kolbe Action Modes® are observable behaviors driven by instinct, not personality or IQ. Instincts are not measurable, but the conative (or Purposeful) acts derived from them are measurable by the Kolbe Index.

The Kolbe A™ Index produces a four-number result representing an individual’s natural way of taking action in four different behavior patterns, or Kolbe Action Modes®

  • Fact Finder − gathering and sharing information

  • Follow Thru − arranging and designing

  • Quick Start − dealing with risk and uncertainty

  • Implementor − handling space and tangibles
